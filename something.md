No one can stop the adventurer. Every world will always recognize your courage and let you pass through the most ridicolous of paths. Whether it is a tunnel of absurdity or a moth cave or something you can't even comprehend. A naive smile can be more powerful than a diamond axe. 

Although the threats and hypnotic voices are always lurking around the next corner I don't know if we have time to pay attention. What if it's a distraction? It must be.

Just remember to notice the orange trees when passing green valleys, the color story once an accident now can be a source of inspiration. 

And of course, be positive and march forward as you always do.
